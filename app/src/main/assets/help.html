• What is this app?

  • True Strength is a fitness app that takes you through a three week training plan created by Michael Johnson.
  • How to use this app?

  • My Profile:

    This tab shows the details that you entered on first startup
    such as your height and weight

    My Exercises

    This is the main chunk of the app. Here, you will go through
    your daily exercises. Each exercise comes with a how-to that shows you how to do each exercise.
  • How can you contact Mike?

  • You can get in touch with Mike by going to the official True Strength Facebook page: True Strength NZ
  • Where can I place feedback or report issues?

  • You can place feedback or report issues on the True Strength Facebook page: True Strength NZ
  • Are there any paid features in this app?

  • No. This app is completely free. Unlike other apps, all features within this app can be used without any extra cost.
  • Privacy Policy

  • We value your privacy and as such, this app doesn't send us any of your information. All information you enter into the app is saved locally, so there is no need for us to store any of your information on the web.
